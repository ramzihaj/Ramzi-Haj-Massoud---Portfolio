# Profile Photo Instructions

## How to Add Your Professional Photo

Your portfolio now displays a professional photo instead of the 3D avatar.

### Required Photo

**Save your professional photo as**: `ramzi-professional.jpg`

### Steps

1. **Choose your best professional photo** (the one you uploaded in chat)
2. **Rename it** to: `ramzi-professional.jpg`
3. **Place it in**: `/public/images/ramzi-professional.jpg`

### Photo Requirements

- **Format**: JPG or JPEG
- **Name**: `ramzi-professional.jpg` (exact name, lowercase)
- **Recommended Size**: 800x1000 pixels or similar portrait aspect ratio
- **Quality**: High resolution (at least 1MB for crisp display)
- **Background**: Clean, professional background (like the one in your photo)
